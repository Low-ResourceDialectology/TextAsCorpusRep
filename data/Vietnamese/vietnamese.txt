- 2022NgoSynthetic → [[Ngo et al., 2022 - Synthetic Data]]
Name: English - Vietnamese, French - Vietnamese, Chinese - Vietnamese and Japanese - Vietnamese bilingual corpus for Neural Machine Translation
Authors: Ngo, Thi-Vinh and Nguyen, Phuong-Thai and Ha, Thanh-Le and Dinh, Khac-Quy and Nguyen, Le-Minh
Description: The data is collected from TED talks.
URL: https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/08839514.2022.2101755 (Paper)
Github: https://github.com/ngovinhtn/Low-resource-Machine-Translation
Content: English-Vietnamese, French-Vietnamese, Chinese-Vietnamese, Japanese-Vietnamese sentence pairs
Size: 223,825 Eng-Vie, 205,396 Fra-Vie, 246688 Zho-Vie, 245,905 Jap-Vie
Note: This work collected bilingual datasets Chinese-Vietnamese, Japanese-Vietnamese from the TED Talks domain and release at 1. https://github.com/ngovinhtn/Low-resourceMachine-Translation.git 2. www.phontron.com/kytea/ 3. https://taku910.github.io/mecab/ 4. https://spacy.io/ 5. https://opus.nlpl.eu/CCAligned.php 6. https://github.com/quanpn90/NMTGMinor 7. https://github.com/google-research/bert 8. https://github.com/mjpost/sacrebleu 9. https://www.ted.com/talks 10. https://www2.nict.go.jp/astrec-att/member/mutiyama/ALT/ 11. https://github.com/fxsjy/jieba 12. https://github.com/moses-smt/mosesdecoder/tree/master/scripts
Access: git clone git@github.com:ngovinhtn/Low-resource-Machine-Translation.git

- 2021DoanPhoMT/ → [[Doan et al., 2021 - PhoMT Vietnamese-English]]
Name: PhoMT: A High-Quality and Large-Scale Benchmark Dataset for Vietnamese-English Machine Translation
Authors: Long Doan and Linh The Nguyen and Nguyen Luong Tran and Thai Hoang and Dat Quoc Nguyen
Description: PhoMT is a high-quality and large-scale Vietnamese-English parallel dataset of 3.02M sentence pairs.
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.12199 (Paper)
Github: https://github.com/VinAIResearch/PhoMT#phomt-a-high-quality-and-large-scale-benchmark-dataset-for-vietnamese-english-machine-translation
Content: Vietnamese and English sentence pairs
Size: 3,020,000 pairs
Note: 
Access: Prior to download, this form has to be filled out: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfShfLzQ3w9ErBc6sId55s83o9tOv2qz6zMd_6lNCD7n791NQ/viewform including agreement to (1) to use PhoMT for research or educational purposes only, (2) to not distribute PhoMT or part of PhoMT in any original or modified form, (3) and to cite the EMNLP 2021 paper "PhoMT: A High-Quality and Large-Scale Benchmark Dataset for Vietnamese-English Machine Translation" whenever PhoMT is employed to help produce published results.
